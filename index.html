Help Save Baby Omar from Respiratory Distress Syndrome
Baby Omar was born prematurely and is fighting for his life in the NICU. He’s been diagnosed with Respiratory Distress Syndrome (RDS), a condition that
makes it difficult for him to breathe on his own.
To survive, Omar urgently needs a ventilator and specialized care. His parents are doing everything they can, but the mounting medical bills are
overwhelming.
Your support can help provide the critical care Omar needs to grow stronger each day. Every donation brings him closer to a healthy future.
